A Memorable Birthday
May 7th, 2023: The Proposal
A year later, on May 7th, 2023, my birthday was destined to be even more memorable. We planned a lunch date to celebrate. As we enjoyed our meal and the conversation flowed, Ayokanmi did something I didn't expect. With a mix of humor and seriousness, he asked, “WILL YOU MARRY ME? PLEASE TAKE YOUR TIME TO ANSWER.” My heart raced. I was so surprised and unsure of what to say at that moment. I needed time to process this huge step. On May 19th, I sent him a message saying “YES,” and on May 22nd, I reaffirmed my answer in person. That moment solidified our future together.


Building Our Future Together
The journey from that initial conversation to the proposal was filled with numerous moments of joy and challenges. Sharing our dreams, aspirations, and goals required effort and understanding. There were times when it was difficult, but we always leaned on each other and our faith in God. Making God the foundation of our relationship has been crucial.
His guidance has brought us closer and helped us navigate the ups and downs of life together. The counseling we received from family and mentors was invaluable. They provided us with wisdom and support that strengthened our bond.

A Message to Future Couples
To all the intending couples out there, we cannot stress enough the importance of making God the cornerstone of your relationship. Without Him, a life and a family have nothing. “A LIFE AND A FAMILY WITH JESUS HAVE EVERYTHING.” Our story is a testament to the power of faith, friendship, and love. We are excited about our future together and thankful for every moment that brought us to this point. As we look forward to our life together, we remain grateful for the journey we've had and for the people who have supported us along the way. Our story is still being written, and we know that with God’s guidance, the best is yet to come. Cheers.
Abegunde Rebecca Monjolajesu
