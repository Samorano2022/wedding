Our story: A Love journey

Bride's Love Journey

December 1st, 2021: The church welfare posted his picture on the church platform, and I decided to wish him a happy birthday in his private chat. Surprisingly, he replied almost immediately, and we chatted from 8:00 AM till 3:00 PM. It was funny how we shared many of our childhood experiences and growing up that same day. May 7th, 2022: He came to my school for my birthday, and I took him to the best Amala joint in my school. We had fun, and he left for Ibadan around 6:00 PM. That was one of my best birthdays ever because I got to share it with my new best friend. I started falling in love with him from that day and knew he was the perfect person for me. Ayokanmi is caring, has a big heart, and is very accommodating. May 7th, 2023: On my birthday, we went out for a lunch date. After the date, he popped the big question in a funny way: “WILL YOU MARRY ME? PLEASE TAKE YOUR TIME TO ANSWER.” I was so surprised because I was unsure of what to say. I replied with a message saying “YES” on May 19th, and on May 22nd, I replied with a “YES” in person. Sharing our dreams, aspirations, and goals and envisioning them together was a bit challenging, but God helped us in His infinite mercy. One thing that has helped and continues to help is making God the foundation of this relationship. Special thanks to all the counseling we have received so far and the family that has counseled us as an intended couple. Your counseling has helped us greatly. Finally, to the intending couples out there, please make God the foundation of your relationship. Remember, a life and a family without Jesus have nothing. “A LIFE AND A FAMILY WITH JESUS HAVE EVERYTHING.”

Rebecca Monjolajesu
