Groom's Love Journey

I noticed her immediately but pretended to be indifferent about my feelings and looked away, thinking she was her sister's firstborn. So, I dismissed my initial feelings.

Our first conversation was on WhatsApp. Here’s how it happened: on the 1st of December 2021, which happens to be my birthday, a few days after our visit to their house, she saw the birthday wishes and my picture on the church platform. She decided to slide into my DMs to send her birthday wishes, thank me for the visit, and send a gift.

We started chatting, and our discussions were often full of funny moments. I discovered she liked expressing herself and talking about her day, especially about her lectures since she was still an undergraduate at that time.
