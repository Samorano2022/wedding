Rebecca

December 1st, 2021: A Surprising Connection
It was a typical day until the church welfare committee posted Ayokanmi’s picture on our church platform. As I glanced at his picture, something compelled me to reach out and wish him a happy birthday. I sent a quick message in his private chat, not expecting much. To my surprise, he replied almost immediately. That spontaneous conversation lasted from 8:00 AM till 3:00 PM. We laughed and shared many of our childhood experiences and stories about growing up. It was astonishing how much we had in common, and an unexpected bond began to form between us.

May 7th, 2022: A Birthday to Remember
Fast forward to May 7th, 2022. It was my birthday, and Ayokanmi decided to make it special by coming to my school. I was excited and slightly nervous as I took him to the best Amala joint around. We enjoyed a wonderful meal, sharing stories and laughter. He had to leave for Ibadan around 6:00 PM, but those few hours together were magical. It was one of the best birthdays I had ever had, not just because of the fun, but because I got to spend it with my new best friend. How We Met
We first met at her sister’s house during a sick visit in 2021. They weren't at home, so we had to wait for a while. Suddenly, a beautiful damsel walked in with her sister. I noticed her but pretended to be indifferent about my feelings and looked away because I was calculating, thinking Monjolajesu was her sister's firstborn, so I dismissed my feelings.

Our First Conversation
Our first real conversation happened on WhatsApp. It was December 1st, 2021 my birthday just a few days after our visit to their house. Monjolajesu saw the birthday wishes and my picture on the church platform and decided to slide into my DMs to send her greetings and thank me for our visit. That simple gesture sparked an instant connection. We started chatting, and our first conversation was about our upbringing, environment, and culture. Sharing our past that day was a pivotal moment for me. The topics we discussed drew me in and made me reflect on my own life. I even shared my goals with her, and she offered encouraging words that resonated deeply. At one point, I asked her age not out of disrespect, but because I was genuinely curious. Although she didn't declare her exact age, she gave me a range, and I was surprised to find she was older than I initially thought. The initial feelings I had dismissed came rushing back.
